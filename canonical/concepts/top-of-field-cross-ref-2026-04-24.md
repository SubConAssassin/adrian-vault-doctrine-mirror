## 0. Method + authenticity guard

**One ChatGPT or Grok API call per business** (Adrian's spend protocol = one-shot per question). The API selects 3 top operators with substantive name recognition; each gets one ADOPT and one REJECT recommendation. Claude (this loop) layers the P1–P15 lens on top of the API output.

**Authenticity rule (non-negotiable):** every recommendation is marked **adopt** / **consider** / **reject for these reasons**. No silent imposition of industry templates. Where Adrian's voice (per `adrian-corpus/voice-profile.md`) deliberately diverges from the operator pattern, that divergence is honoured and the rejection is stated.

**Hard-rule guard active throughout:** no Chelsea, no Cosmic Vein, no 2500C, no grown-in-Siberia, no teardrop pendant, no Arcturian/diamond-wire-saw in marketing-pattern recommendations.

## 1. OSB — Original Siberian Blue (luxury spiritual jewellery)

**ChatGPT (gpt-5.4-mini) selected the 3 closest psychographic-fit operators** — symbolic, provenance-led, high-AOV, identity/status jewellery (not mass crystal retail):

- **REJECT — for these reasons:** **Foundrae's over-philosophical marketing voice.** Foundrae stays premium partly by letting symbols carry meaning without sounding like a wellness brand or making grand claims. **OSB does not need to copy that quietness — Adrian's register is more direct, more philosophical, more confident.** The OSB customer wants to hear Adrian explain Arcturian frequency in the blue electromagnetic bandwidth; Foundrae would dial it down. Per `adrian-corpus/voice-profile.md`, Adrian's authority comes from being explicit, not understated. Reject the dilution.

### 1.2 Pyrrha

- **ADOPT:** **Antique-/seal-style provenance storytelling for each motif.** Pyrrha makes every piece feel like it has lineage — sometimes literal (recovered from Victorian wax seals), sometimes mythic. **OSB has the strongest provenance story in the category** — laboratory outside Moscow, ~220 kg supply globally, GIA Koivula 1993 paper, Stephan Schwartz lineage — and currently underexploits it. Reject the temptation to write copy that "could apply to any of our pieces" — write copy specific to each piece's role in the symbol system.

### 1.3 Cathy Waterman

- **ADOPT:** **Handcrafted delicacy paired with quiet luxury pricing discipline.** Waterman demonstrates that spiritual / nature-inflected jewellery can sit firmly in luxury when the execution feels artisan, restrained, and unmistakably high-end. The pricing never apologises; the work speaks. **OSB application:** lean into the "4 days per pendant, master artisan in Bali (Yoga)" register. The price tier ($400–$1,118) is correct for the work; defend it without justification. Per `sales-language.md` §11: state the number, pause, let the prospect decide. Never volunteer a discount until interest is qualified. Waterman's pricing register confirms this practice as luxury-category orthodox.

- **REJECT — for these reasons:** **Trend-chasing product architecture.** Waterman releases new collections seasonally, riding luxury fashion cycles. **OSB should not dilute its science-provenance story with seasonal fashion volatility.** The OSB brand needs **permanence, not novelty cycles.** New variants (Merkaba, Tranquility, Arcturian Navigator) should land as additions to the canonical symbol system, not as "Spring 2027 collection". Reject the seasonal cadence; embrace permanence.

### 1.4 P1–P15 lens application for OSB

- **P4 (decisive product focus):** Foundrae's symbol-system discipline reinforces this. OSB's flagship is the Arcturian Soul Purpose Amulet — every other variant exists in relation to it, not as a standalone fashion item.
- **P5 (insist on quality):** Cathy Waterman validates the "4 days per pendant, never apologise on price" stance. Don't ship anything below standard to hit a price point.
- **P6 (plan for sustained hardship):** all three top operators are decade+-old businesses. Build OSB on that horizon — testimonial library compounding, symbol catalogue compounding, customer-collection-over-years arc.
- **P12 (concentrated bets, durable moats):** the symbol system + provenance lineage + finite-supply story are three compounding moats. Don't fragment energy across thin spread.
- **P13 (work backwards from customer):** what does the OSB-customer-testimonial-2-years-out say? Best inference from corpus + this analysis: *"I have three of his pieces now; each one anchors a different season of who I'm becoming. The card that came with the first one is on my altar."* If that's the testimonial, the symbol-system + provenance-card recommendations both serve it directly.

### 1.5 Best strategic takeaway for OSB (synthesised)
