## The Five Methodology IP Assets

### IP-1: The Subconscious Surgery Four-Phase Protocol

**Owner:** Adrian Taffinder, personally.
**Used in:** SS (primary practice), Bodhisvara (reference methodology for therapist matching), training content in Mind Expansion Academy, Ashta (group decision frameworks borrow from this).

**The four phases:**

1. **Identification** — Diagnosing which limiting belief or subconscious block is currently load-bearing for the client. The diagnostic instrument is language. The client's word choice, syntax, and framing reveal their relationship with the issue. The signature principle: "Language is my scalpel."

2. **Extraction** — Locating the originating story sustaining the limiting identity. This is rarely the story the client thinks it is. The therapist works backwards from current symptoms through the linguistic patterns to the moment a foundational meaning was made. Extraction is precision work — too shallow and the belief reforms; too deep and the client's defences fire.

3. **The Prescription (Narrative Resculpting)** — A pattern interrupt that removes the emotional charge from the originating story without erasing the memory. The client retains the experience but loses the meaning that was crippling them. The new meaning is co-created, never imposed.

4. **Activation** — A bespoke action plan that allows the client to live from the new identity. Without activation, the work is theoretical. Activation is short-feedback-loop behaviours that prove the new identity in real conditions.

**Delivery model:** Remote. 10-hour retainer blocks with 24/7 access. Surrogate muscle testing means Adrian tests on behalf of the client (the client's body responds through Adrian's). Average client relationship: 3+ years.

**What is uniquely Adrian's:**
- The four-phase sequencing (other practitioners use components, none use this exact arc)
- The language-as-diagnostic principle ("Language is my scalpel")
- Surrogate muscle testing protocol (remote, not in-person)
- The specific use of pattern interrupt + narrative resculpting in phase 3 (most coaches stop at insight; Adrian completes the rewrite)

**What is NOT uniquely Adrian's (do not claim):**
- Muscle testing as a category (kinesiology is decades old)
- Pattern interrupts (NLP standard)
- The general concept of subconscious work

**Governance recommendation:** Owned by Adrian personally. Licensed to:
- SS (free use, this is the practice)
- Bodhisvara (reference methodology — therapist evaluations against this protocol)
- Ashta (group decision-making principles only — not the full four-phase protocol)
- Any future SS-derived training products require explicit Adrian licence

### IP-2: The Three-Layer Testing Engine

**Owner:** Adrian Taffinder, personally. **Critical to keep outside any project's cap table.**
**Used in:** SS (manual), Ashta (core feature), Bodhisvara (voice layer), TUNED (proposed).

**The three layers:**

1. **Conscious opinion** — the person states what they think the answer is on a 1-10 scale. Captures cognitive bias, desire, fear, social-desirability distortion.

2. **Self muscle testing** — galvanic skin response or finger-method kinesiology. Bypasses the conscious mind. Captures the body's reading.

3. **Phone-based biometric** — accelerometer (Sensie API protocol: 3-shake) and/or voice-analytics (HRV via PPG + vocal biomarkers). Provides an instrument-mediated objective baseline.

**The product insight is the delta between the three signals.** Congruence (all three agree) signals truth. Incongruence flags exactly which kind of distortion is operating: conscious bias if opinion diverges from body+instrument; suppression if body diverges from opinion+instrument; instrument error if instrument diverges from opinion+body.

**Group extension:** all members test independently and submit blindly-then-simultaneously. Weighted average reveals collective truth; outliers are flagged for individual exploration. This is the "Clubhouse mechanic" Adrian pioneered live.

**Engineering constraint:** the three signals cannot be captured simultaneously. Motion artifacts (phone shaking) corrupt PPG by 10× the cardiac signal; handling noise corrupts voice. **Sequential capture is mandatory** — Phase 1 PPG (20-30s, phone stationary), Phase 2 voice (5-10s, phone still), Phase 3 accelerometer (5-10s, in hand). Total per measurement: 55-75s.

**What is uniquely Adrian's:**
- The three-layer architecture (opinion / self-test / instrument as distinct epistemological sources)
- The delta-as-product-insight principle (not the readings themselves but the gaps between them)
- The blind-simultaneous group submission UX
- The application to consciousness work specifically (Sensie was built for sports/wellness, not for inner work)

**What is NOT uniquely Adrian's:**
- HRV via PPG (well-established)
- Accelerometer as a kinesiology proxy (Sensie's invention)
- Voice biomarkers (commercial offerings exist)

**Governance recommendation — CRITICAL:**
- IP held by Adrian personally OR an Adrian-controlled holding entity (e.g. UK Ltd).
- Licensed in to SS (no fee, internal use), Ashta (royalty or equity-as-licence-fee), Bodhisvara (TBD).
- **Must NOT be inside Ashta's cap table.** If Ashta's 8 co-founders own the engine, Adrian loses control of his own diagnostic methodology in every other product.
- Architecture document to be commissioned (Antigravity scope, see `working/handoffs/2026-04-18-ecosystem-canonical-built.md`).

### IP-5: The Surrogate / Group Testing Protocols

**Owner:** Adrian Taffinder, personally.
**Used in:** SS (1:1 surrogate testing, Adrian tests on client's behalf), Ashta (group testing with blind-simultaneous submission), past Clubhouse demos.

**The two protocols:**

**Surrogate testing:** the practitioner's body acts as the diagnostic instrument while holding the client's energetic signal. Adrian uses this to test remotely (no in-person required). The practitioner's calibration baseline (truth/falsehood/uncertainty) must be established before any client work.

**Group testing with blind-simultaneous submission:** all members test independently, write their answer in chat but do not send. Practitioner confirms readiness from each. All members send simultaneously on signal. Outliers are visible immediately and flagged for follow-up. Average is the group truth. This eliminates the herd-influence problem that plagues normal group surveys and decision-making.

**What is uniquely Adrian's:**
- The remote surrogate protocol with explicit calibration baseline
- The Clubhouse-derived simultaneous submission UX (and its applicability to digital implementation in Ashta)
- The integration of group testing with collective investment / decision frameworks (the Ashta cooperative hedge fund)

**What is NOT uniquely Adrian's:**
- Surrogate testing as a category (used in many kinesiology lineages)
- Group decision-making (vast literature)

**Governance recommendation:** Owned by Adrian. The simultaneous-submission UX should be specifically documented and treated as patentable subject matter when funds permit. (Not patent-blocking work today; just file-it-away-for-later.)
