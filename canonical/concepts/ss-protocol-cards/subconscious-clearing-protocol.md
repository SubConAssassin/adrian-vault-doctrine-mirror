# Protocol: Subconscious Clearing Protocol

* **Alchemical Root (Karol Truman):** Utilizes the core emotional mapping principles of *Feelings Buried Alive Never Die* (FEBAND) to trace physical symptoms directly to suppressed emotional patterns.
* **Clinical Transition (Dr John DeVore):** Dr John taught Adrian to structure these emotional associations into clear somatic indicators and run weekly clearing loops during their Skype training from 2014 to 2015.
* **Modality Mastery (Adrian Alan Taffinder):** Adrian synthesized these practices to create his signature 15-line clearing script, designed to bypass the prefrontal cortex, resolve the "Safe Route Pathology," and release unhealed trauma stored in the body's energy centers.

## §2 Apparatus

1. **Participants:** The Practitioner (Surgeon) and the Client (Operator), or the Primary Operator performing self-clearing.
2. **The Somatic Tap Points:** 
   * **Stomach Meridian (Primary Point):** Located directly beneath the pupils on the cheekbones. Tapping this point stimulates the stomach meridian, which governs the autonomic "startle response" and represents the origin point of all somatic fear.

3. **The Cohesive Statement Script:** The 15-line verbatim surgical script or a customized, practitioner-authored cohesive statement cascading through the client's specific blocks.

## §3 Method (step-by-step)

### Step 1: Target Diagnostic Identification
Before executing the clearance, the practitioner must run muscle-testing and timeline scans to isolate the exact energetic coordinates:
1. Identify the suppressed emotion (e.g. *unforgiveness, resentment, anger*).
2. Identify the biological storage organ (e.g. *liver, heart, throat*).
3. Identify the age node where the trauma was anchored (e.g. *Womb, Age 5, Age 10*).

### Step 2: Codifying the Cohesive Statement
The practitioner dictates the custom cohesive statement. The client writes it down by hand to engage motor-sensory pathways. The statement must cascade through six rigid logical layers:
1. **Absolute Self-Acceptance:** *"I completely love and accept myself..."* (Grounds the nervous system in safety).
2. **Present Emotional State:** Acknowledging the current friction (e.g. *"Even if my inner child is in turmoil..."*).
3. **Somatic Location:** Linking the emotional blockage to its biological organ (e.g. *"...storing this conflict in my liver..."*).
4. **Immediate Behavioral Cause:** Revealing the pattern (e.g. *"...due to wanting to control all immediate outcomes..."*).
5. **The Self-Sabotage Loop:** Exposing the reaction (e.g. *"...which makes me constantly second-guess every decision..."*).
6. **The Core Reframe:** The final awakening phrase (e.g. *"...which I now realise is anger I hold for myself because I don't trust my own capacity..."*).

### Step 3: Pattern Interrupt & Tap Stimulation
1. The client places the index and middle fingers of both hands on the cheekbones directly beneath the eyes (stomach meridian tap points).
2. The client begins tapping with a steady, moderate rhythm (approximately 2-3 taps per second).
3. Tapping continues continuously throughout the reading of the script to keep the prefrontal cortex occupied and stimulate the energetic meridian.

### Step 4: Script Vocalisation (6 Repetitions)
1. The client reads the cohesive statement or 15-line surgical script aloud, maintaining a steady, calm tone.
2. The practitioner monitors the client's physical indicators, looking for somatic signs of release (yawning, sighing, spontaneous tears, flushing, or sudden shifts in breathing depth).
3. The script is read aloud **exactly 6 times** while tapping. Six is the master frequency used in Subconscious Surgery to bypass logical resistance.

### Step 5: Somatic Grounding and Integration
Immediately following the sixth repetition, the practitioner executes the **Gravity Grounding Protocol** to safely discharge the displaced trauma down through the feet, preventing energetic overload:
1. Command the client: *"Right, take a nice long slow deep breath. Fill your chest, fill your lungs, fill your belly."*
2. Command: *"And on the out breath just ground all that energy. Allow it to drain out of your body, like gravity's pulling it down and it's draining out of your feet into the floor."*
3. Repeat the grounding breath three times. Re-test the somatic baseline to confirm the block is cleared.

## §4 When Used

* **Phase 2 (Subconscious Clearing):** The core active step in every 1:1 working session to dissolve identified blocks.

## §5 Common Pitfalls

1. **Skipping the Hook (Self-Acceptance):** Initiating the script without the mandatory self-acceptance line (*"I completely love and accept myself"*) will trigger the limbic system, causing the client to experience intense resistance, throat tightness, or immediate somatic shutdown.
2. **Insufficient Grounding:** If the gravity grounding protocol is not executed thoroughly, the displaced emotional energy remains congested in the upper torso, resulting in physical headaches, nausea, or profound physical exhaustion.
3. **Fluffy Reframes:** In custom cohesive statements, failing to formulate an honest, confronting "I now realise" reframe prevents the client from taking sovereign responsibility, rendering the clearing superficial.
