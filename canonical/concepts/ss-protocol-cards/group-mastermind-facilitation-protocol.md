## §3 Method (step-by-step)

### Step 1: Collective Heart Field Entrainment
1. The facilitator opens the mastermind session by guiding all participants to sit in complete stillness, shoulders relaxed, breathing in a slow, rhythmic pattern.
2. The facilitator utilizes HeartMath coherence principles, broadcasting a state of deep self-acceptance to physically entrain the cardiorespiratory rhythms of the group.
3. Establish a shared zero-point field, dissolving external business, relational, or domestic friction.

### Step 2: Individual Focused Diagnostics (The Proxy Scan)
1. The facilitator selects a volunteer from the cohort who is experiencing an acute, representative bottleneck (e.g. *chronic procrastination, launch fear, financial self-sabotage*).
2. The facilitator performs direct muscle-testing and chronological timeline scans on the volunteer. The volunteer acts as an energetic "proxy" or focusing lens for the collective blockages of the group.
3. The specific biological organ and age of trauma (e.g. *Transverse Colon | Analytical Resistance | Age 10*) are identified.

### Step 3: Group-Synchronised Repetition
1. The facilitator crafts the clearing cohesive statement or dictates the signature **15-Line Clearing Script** line-by-line.
2. The entire cohort is instructed to place their fingers on the stomach meridian tap points under the eyes.
3. The facilitator reads a line, and the **entire cohort repeats the line in synchronous unison**.
4. The script is vocalized **exactly 6 times**. The collective auditory and somatic resonance completely overwhelms individual analytical resistance, dragging all participants into a deep, receptive theta wave state where subconscious reprogramming occurs.

### Step 4: Collective Gravity Grounding
1. Immediately following the sixth repetition, the facilitator guides the entire cohort through the gravity grounding protocol: *"Take a nice long slow deep breath. Fill your lungs. And on the out breath just ground all that energy, allowing it to drain out of your feet into the floor."*
2. Execute three synchronized grounding breaths to discharge the massive kinetic and emotional energy released by the group clearing.

### Step 5: Somatic Locking with the Phoenix Pendant
1. For participants in the advanced tiers, the facilitator commands them to touch or hold their **Phoenix Pendant**.
2. The facilitator guides a visualization linking the weight and deep alchemical color of the hydrothermal Siberian Blue quartz directly to the cleared, sovereign state of consciousness just achieved.
