# Protocol: Intake Session Structure

## §1 Origin + Lineage

The intake session structure represents the foundational gateway of the **Subconscious Surgery™** client journey. It was codified by Adrian Alan Taffinder during the early years of his commercial coaching practice to onboard clients rapidly and safely into the somatic-testing model:

* **Practice Ingest:** Across 28 captured client sessions in the **2019 practice corpus**, Adrian systematically executed this precise seven-stage session arc, refining the onboarding language to bridge the gap between logical business strategy and deep energetic clearing.
* **Modality Boundary:** The structure incorporates strict safeguards to prevent the unlicensed practice of therapy, medical diagnostics, or legal representation, establishing a sovereign, non-advisory coaching container from the first minute of engagement.

Verbatim grounding in the corpus:
> "What I do is quite unusual but it works... When you were born, you had an operating system. You had software installed through experience. As you get older, experiences get more complex but they all carry a description of pain that carries the energy... This is the surgery reference — I remove the block"

## §2 Apparatus

1. **Participants:** The Practitioner (conducting the intake) and the Client (entering the coaching journey).
2. **Physical / Digital Setup:** A secure, private space. High-definition audio/video capability. Practitioner has a notepad or digital interface to capture client language patterns silently.
3. **The Calibration Instruments:** Kinesiology muscle-testing systems (sway or finger loop) to establish autonomic responses.
4. **Curriculum Material:** The initial **Affirmation Roster** templates and the **1-10 Quantifiable SMART Scale**.

## §3 Method (step-by-step)

The intake session is executed across a rigid, highly structured **Seven-Stage Arc**:

### Stage 1: Raport & Opening Check-in (Minutes 1-10)
1. Establish a warm, supportive rapport. Discuss general lifestyle parameters, current personal coordinates, and short-term updates.
2. Introduce the sovereign coaching container, reminding the client that they are the absolute author of their reality, and that the practitioner offers perspectives and intuitive feedback, not advice or instructions.

### Stage 2: The "Download" or "Brain Dump" (Minutes 10-25)
1. Invite the client to share their current challenge: *"Right, give me a download of what's going on, or just do a brain dump."*
2. The client speaks without structure. The practitioner listens in deep silence, screen off if on video, silently making notes of recurring linguistic cues, comfort-zone phrases, self-sabotaging structures, and "victim loop" statements (e.g. *"he took my peace of mind"*).

### Stage 3: Autonomic Baseline Calibration (Minutes 25-35)
1. transition to active somatic testing. Explain the kinesiology feedback system: *"When you were born, you had an operating system... What I do is quite unusual but it works. I'm going to do some testing using my phone/muscle loops."*
2. Calibrate the client's physiology: Guide them to state absolute truths (*"My name is..."*) vs absolute lies (*"My name is Steve"*). Verify that the autonomic circuit is unswitched and responsive.

### Stage 4: Translating Subjective Blocks (Minutes 35-45)
1. Take the client's vague, emotional complaints captured during the brain dump and reframe them into quantifiable, time-bound inquiries.
2. Apply the **1-10 Quantifiable SMART Scale**: *"On a score of 1 to 10, will launching [Project X] result in a functional improvement in your business, where 5 is neutral, 1 is total ruin, and 10 is exponential growth?"*
3. somatic-test the inquiries, mapping the exact numerical coordinates of their current blocks.

### Stage 5: Initial Somatic Scan & Case Diagnostic (Minutes 45-55)
1. Perform the biological coordinate scan to locate where the emotional block is stored in the body (e.g. *Liver, Heart, Throat, Transverse Colon*).
2. Execute the **Age-Regression Scan**, methodically checking the five milestones (Womb, Age 5, Age 10, Age 20, Age 30) to pinpoint the exact year the blockage was anchored.

### Stage 6: Formulating and Clearing the Cohesive Statement (Minutes 55-70)
1. Dictate the custom cohesive statement to the client, incorporating the identified organ, age, unhealed feeling, and reframe phrase. The client writes it down verbatim.
2. Execute the pattern interrupt: The client taps the stomach meridian points under the eyes while reading the statement aloud **exactly 6 times**.
3. Complete the **Gravity Grounding Protocol** to safely discharge the displaced trauma down through the feet.

### Stage 7: Affirmation Roster Update & Spacing (Minutes 70-80)
1. Onboard the client onto their first **Affirmation Roster** (typically 6-8 new lines, repeated 6 times daily).
2. Establish the spacing: Schedule the first follow-up working session **exactly two weeks out**. Remind the client: *"We space the sessions by two weeks — that is for the energetic processing of the clearing."* Confirm that short check-ins are available if needed.

## §4 When Used

* **First Session:** The mandatory template for the very first 1:1 interaction with a prospective client.
* **Re-onboarding:** When a prior client returns after a long coaching hiatus to establish a fresh baseline.

## §5 Common Pitfalls

1. **Jumping Straight to Clearing:** Attempting to run the subconscious clearing script in Stage 6 without establishing the Stage 3 somatic baseline results in immediate failure, as the client's logical resistance remains active.
2. **Giving Advice:** The practitioner slips into giving business, legal, or therapeutic advice during the Stage 2 "Download," violating the non-advisory register and creating dependency.
3. **Fluffy Pacing:** Allowing the client to talk for the entire session without transitioning to the structured calibrations. The "Download" must be strictly time-boxed to 15 minutes.
