## 0. What this is

**The Mastermind program's January 2022 cohort enrollment roster, with cross-corpus presence resolved.**

## 1. Cohort scale finding

**Previously (per the 2026-05-17 speaker-map):** the Mastermind program was understood to have **9 recurring-named-student participants** (Ashley, Dorinda, Guinevere, Latonya/Latanya, Marian, Remelle, Rhonda, Tiffany, Trinia + Emme as separate).

The 9-name speaker-map was the subset who anchored their own Mastermind audio segments via filename — i.e. the active, audio-producing subset of the cohort. The 65-name roster includes all enrollees regardless of in-cohort visibility.

**Scale correction:** the Mastermind program is materially larger than the speaker-map suggested. Multiple cohort cycles × ~65 enrollees per cohort = hundreds of paying clients across the program's life. Substantial business / clinical / legal context.
