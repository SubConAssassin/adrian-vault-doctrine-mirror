## §2 The Question-Loop Structure (Open → Excursion → Synthesis)

Adrian structures his coaching interactions using a dynamic, recursive linguistic framework known as the **Question-Loop Structure**. This pattern is designed to move the client systematically from conscious, analytical thinking (beta state) into the deep, intuitive subconscious (theta state), before returning them to conscious integration.

This structure operates across three distinct phases:

```
[ PHASE 1: THE OPEN ]
"What's coming up? Give me a download."
(Analytical Beta State)
         |
         v
[ PHASE 2: THE EXCURSION ]
"Let's test that... where is it stored?"
(Somatic/Chronological Inquiries)
         |
         v
[ PHASE 3: THE SYNTHESIS ]
"I completely love and accept myself, even if..."
(Verbatim Reframe & Grounding)
```

### Phase 1: The Open
The opening loop begins by establishing immediate rapport and asking the client for an unstructured, honest brain-dump.
* **Key Facilitation Phrases:**
  - *"What's coming up for you today?"*
  - *"Give me a download on where you're at."*
  - *"Let's do a mind-dump on the current friction."*
* **Linguistic Goal:** The practitioner does not interrupt or attempt to solve the problem during this phase. They actively make notes of recurring emotional themes, looking for specific linguistic triggers (e.g. *"I have to make the right choice,"* *"They are taking my peace of mind"*).

### Phase 2: The Excursion
Once the download is complete, the practitioner interrupts the logical narrative and initiates a somatic and chronological excursion.
* **Key Facilitation Phrases:**
  - *"Right. Let's take the logic out of this. What does the system say?"*
  - *"Let's test it on a scale of 1 to 10."*
  - *"Where is that stored in the body? Is it liver? Is it heart? Let's check."*
  - *"Chronological scan. Let's check the milestones. Womb? Age 5? Age 10? Where was this anchored?"*
* **Linguistic Goal:** By shifting the focus from the logical story (*"what happened in my business today"*) to physical sensations (*"where is this stored"*), the client's prefrontal cortex is placed offline. The practitioner guides the client through deep chronological scans to locate the emotional origin point, bypassing defensive ego structures.

### Phase 3: The Synthesis
The excursion concludes by wrapping the discovered somatic and historical patterns into a unified, Custom Cohesive Statement.
* **Key Facilitation Phrases:**
  - *"Okay, here is your statement. Write this down exactly as I dictate it."*
  - *"Now, read it aloud. We're going to loop this six times while tapping under the eyes."*
  - *"Which I now realise is [Reframe]..."*
* **Linguistic Goal:** The Custom Cohesive Statement functions as a linguistic software patch. It combines the raw emotional data uncovered during the excursion into a structured, self-accepting reframe loop. The physical tapping during the repetition restructures the autonomous neurological pathways, permanent clearing the old survival pattern.
