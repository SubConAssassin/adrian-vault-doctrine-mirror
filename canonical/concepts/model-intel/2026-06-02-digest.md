### SEO/GEO — Ahrefs reverses on schema; first 200 words are everything

- **🔴 SCHEMA REVERSAL — Ahrefs Aug 2025 → Mar 2026 controlled study (1,885 pages adding JSON-LD)** found a **statistically significant 4.6% DECREASE in Google AI Overview citations after schema was added**, and no major uplift on any platform. SearchVIU tested 5 major AI systems (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Mode) — **all extracted visible HTML only, ignored JSON-LD entirely** during direct retrieval. Last week's "FAQPage is the cited schema type" framing is **CONTESTED at minimum, probably wrong**. → Sharpen SS GEO strategy away from schema-as-lever toward **visible HTML content quality + answer-in-first-200-words**. [CONFIRMED — [Ahrefs schema study](https://ahrefs.com/blog/schema-ai-citations/), [Elevarus analysis](https://elevarus.com/schema-markup-ai-citations-ahrefs-study-may-2026/)]
- **AI Overviews CTR has stabilised + partially recovered**: bottomed at **1.3% in Dec 2025**, climbed to **2.4% in Feb 2026** (85% jump in two months per Seer Interactive). But Ahrefs still measures **58% lower CTR on top-ranking page when AI Overview present**. AI Overviews appear in **48% of all searches in 2026** (up from 34.5% in Dec 2025). [CONFIRMED — [SearchEngineLand recovery study](https://searchengineland.com/google-ai-overviews-ctr-recovery-study-475566), [Ahrefs 58% study](https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-overviews-reduce-clicks-update/)]
- **First 200 words are load-bearing**: **44.2% of LLM citations come from the first 30% of text**, 31.1% middle, 24.7% conclusion. The first 200 words must **directly and completely answer the primary query** — not build up to it. [CONFIRMED — [Enrich Labs GEO 2026](https://www.enrichlabs.ai/blog/generative-engine-optimization-geo-complete-guide-2026), [SearchEngineLand GEO playbook](https://searchengineland.com/mastering-generative-engine-optimization-in-2026-full-guide-469142)]
- **GEO adoption hard numbers**: **97% of digital leaders report positive GEO impact** (Conductor survey); **GEO is now 12% of average digital marketing budgets**; **32% of digital leaders called GEO their top 2026 priority**. **60%+ of all search interactions now involve an AI-generated component**. [CONFIRMED — [Enrich Labs](https://www.enrichlabs.ai/blog/generative-engine-optimization-geo-complete-guide-2026)]
- **E-E-A-T behaves as ranking + AI-visibility filter, not signal**: content must demonstrate **Information Gain** (first-party data, unique human perspectives, expert-led reviews that an LLM cannot synthesise from existing training). Adrian's verbatim client testimonials + Subconscious Surgery case material = exactly the kind of original first-party data that AI can't synthesise — load-bearing for SS GEO strategy. [CONFIRMED — [ClickRank E-E-A-T 2026](https://www.clickrank.ai/e-e-a-t-and-ai/)]
