### SEO — GEO is now mainstream; the Google↔AI overlap has collapsed

- **🆕 AI search is now 12–18% of English informational queries (Q1 2026), up from <2% a year ago** — and **the overlap between top Google links and AI-cited sources has dropped from 70% to below 20%.** Ranking #1 on Google no longer means being cited in the AI answer; GEO is now a distinct discipline, not an SEO add-on. ChatGPT alone processes >1B queries/week. [CONFIRMED — [Frase GEO 2026](https://www.frase.io/blog/what-is-generative-engine-optimization-geo), [Enrich Labs GEO guide](https://www.enrichlabs.ai/blog/generative-engine-optimization-geo-complete-guide-2026), [AI Magicx GEO](https://www.aimagicx.com/blog/generative-engine-optimization-chatgpt-perplexity-2026)]
- **🆕 Citation mechanics (concrete, actionable):** **first 200 words must directly + completely answer the primary query** (retrieval engines judge relevance on opening content); **paragraphs ≤2–3 sentences** (long blocks don't get extracted); **data-backed content gets cited far more than opinion**; build author authority + earn third-party citations + publish consistently. Per-engine preferences are diverging (each AI builds its own citation taste). [CONFIRMED — [Frase](https://www.frase.io/blog/what-is-generative-engine-optimization-geo), [AI Magicx](https://www.aimagicx.com/blog/generative-engine-optimization-chatgpt-perplexity-2026)]
- **Carries:** AI trusts communities + third-party sources (Reddit/YouTube/Wikipedia/analysts) over brand channels → seed presence where AI engines actually cite; GEO content surfaces in AI Overviews within 48–72h *if* it carries original first-party data + schema; derivative (no new numbers) content won't be cited; measure on brand-mentions + conversion quality, not clicks (AI referral traffic <1% even for cited publishers). **Emerging:** agentic search (Operator-class) will reward machine-readable pricing tables / feature comparisons / step-by-step structure for inclusion in agent workflows.
