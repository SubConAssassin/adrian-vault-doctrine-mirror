- **Ahrefs (Apr–May 2026): schema markup has NO measurable impact on AI visibility.** Adding schema produced no major uplift in citations on AI Overviews, AI Mode, or ChatGPT. **Counters the "schema is the GEO foundation" claim.** Exception: **FAQPage schema IS heavily cited** by AI summaries. Operational meaning: don't overinvest in generic schema bloat; FAQPage on key landing pages is the disciplined play. [CONFIRMED — Ahrefs cited via [position.digital AI SEO stats](https://www.position.digital/blog/ai-seo-statistics/), [Almcorp 2026 SEO trends](https://almcorp.com/blog/seo-trends-2026-rank-google-ai-search/)]
- **Princeton GEO study levers (validated): "Statistics Addition," "Cite Sources," "Quotation Addition" drive the biggest citation uplift — up to 40%.** [CONFIRMED — [AI Thinker Lab GEO 2026](https://aithinkerlab.com/generative-engine-optimization-2026/), [Enrich Labs](https://www.enrichlabs.ai/blog/generative-engine-optimization-geo-complete-guide-2026)]
- **AI Overviews in 99.9% of informational keywords; click reduction now ~58% (up from 34.5% last week's figure).** Long-tail (46%) and question queries (57.9%) dominate. The fight is no longer "rank #1" — it's "be cited in the AI summary that ate the click." [CONFIRMED — [position.digital](https://www.position.digital/blog/ai-seo-statistics/)]
- **Content type that wins citations: comparison articles (32.5% of all AI citations), then opinion pieces (10%).** Best-of content, product pages, and guides drive most AI traffic. [CONFIRMED — [Omnibound GEO stats](https://www.omnibound.ai/blog/generative-engine-optimization-statistics)]
