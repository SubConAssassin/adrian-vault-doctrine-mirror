## D. VENTURE TACTICS — Meta ads, subscription funnels, SEO

### Meta — pLTV via CAPI is now the named pattern

- **Predictive LTV (pLTV) bidding** is *the* 2026 architecture for subscription DTC. A trained ML model scores each new customer on **predicted 12-month value** and passes that score to Meta as the conversion value via the Conversions API. Meta's **Andromeda engine** then optimises toward profiles matching high predicted long-term value — not immediate transaction size. Sidesteps the 7-day attribution window entirely. [CONFIRMED — [AdZeta pLTV](https://www.adzeta.io/blog/pltv-bidding-meta-ads-what-actually-works-2026)]
- **Canonical stack for subscription**: Advantage+ Sales + value optimisation enabled + CAPI supplying pLTV scores as conversion values + Value Rules on top for categorical adjustments. Andromeda handles audience discovery + placement; the pLTV signal steers learning toward your highest-value customer profiles. [CONFIRMED — [Birch value optimisation](https://bir.ch/blog/meta-value-optimization)]
- **EMQ (Event Match Quality) is the single highest-impact lever** for Meta value-optimisation underperformance. Target: **Good (6–7.9) or Great (8–10)**. The highest-impact parameters are **hashed email + hashed phone in every CAPI event**. [CONFIRMED — [Birch CAPI deep-dive](https://bir.ch/blog/meta-ads-optimization)]
- **Bottom-funnel retargeting CTA for subscriptions**: lead with **"Subscribe & Save 20%"** as primary CTA — proven pattern for subscription DTC. Combined with maximise-conversion-value bidding, prioritises higher-LTV subscribers over one-time buyers. [CONFIRMED — [Stackmatix funnel guide](https://www.stackmatix.com/blog/meta-ads-funnel-strategy)]
- **Personal-attributes policy** (sharpest 2026 enforcement risk for self-dev) — still escalated from March 2026; indirect framing ("For people managing X" / "Those dealing with Y") flagged like direct "you". **Ad + landing page + image metadata reviewed as one compliance unit.** [CONFIRMED — carries from v1]

### SEO — the AI Overviews paradigm shift

- **AI Overviews reduce organic clicks on the top result by an average of 34.5%.** Visibility now depends less on page position, more on **whether the brand is cited inside the AI-generated summary**. [CONFIRMED — [Adobe Business SEO 2026](https://business.adobe.com/uk/blog/seo-in-2026-fundamentals), [SEOmator stats](https://seomator.com/blog/ai-seo-statistics)]
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)** is the new layer above traditional SEO — optimising for citation inside Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, Perplexity. Four pillars: (1) **Information Gain** = original first-party data; (2) **AEO** = content formatting that AI extractors can pull cleanly; (3) **off-page brand authority** = real E-E-A-T signals; (4) **flawless technical SEO + schema markup**. [CONFIRMED — [press.farm 2026 SEO](https://press.farm/seo-strategy-2026-how-to-rank/), [evergreen.media trends](https://www.evergreen.media/en/guide/seo-this-year/)]
- **2 billion monthly users engage with Google AI Overviews globally** — the shift from clicking links to consuming AI summaries is now mainstream behaviour, not edge case. [CONFIRMED — [SEOmator](https://seomator.com/blog/ai-seo-statistics)]
