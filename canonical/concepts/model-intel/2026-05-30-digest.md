## D. VENTURE TACTICS — Meta ads, subscription funnels, SEO

### Meta — the operational specifics that sharpen last week's pLTV doctrine

- **Cohort revenue (90-day) > in-platform ROAS as the truth metric.** In-platform ROAS reflects the pLTV score *you* injected and will always look better post-implementation. **Measure 90-day cohort revenue per acquired customer to prove the model is accurate.** This is the most important sharpening of the prior week's pLTV-via-CAPI architecture. [CONFIRMED — [Engage Positive UK DTC playbook](https://engagepositive.com/blog/meta-ads-for-dtc-brands/)]
- **Signal-volume floor: ~50 purchase events per week at ad-account level (not per-campaign).** Below that, the algorithm is guessing. **Concrete gate for the SS Mastermind launch** — if Bronze + tripwire don't hit 50 weekly purchases, value-optimisation will underperform regardless of CAPI hygiene. [CONFIRMED — same source]
- **DTC budget split: 70–80% Advantage+ Shopping (prospecting), 10–15% creative testing, 10–20% retargeting.** Standard 2026 UK DTC allocation. Differs from the personal-development funnel by ~10pts (PD needs more retargeting because cycle is longer), but the prospecting weight holds. [CONFIRMED — same source]
- **CAPI with 'Maximum' data sharing recovers 15–30% of purchase events the Pixel misses.** Direct ROAS uplift. EMQ target ≥6, push for 8+. [CONFIRMED — same source]
- **Creative diversity = +32% direct-response efficiency** (official Meta study). The reward is for **radically different angles, tones, archetypes** — not slight variations of the same concept. Forces the 5-angle architecture the SS strategy doc already specifies; rejects the "iterate the winner" instinct alone. [CONFIRMED — [Brandsearch 500+ winning ads](https://brandsearch.co/blog/winning-meta-ad-patterns-2026)]
- **UGC creative outperforms polished studio by ~64% CTR (1.8% vs 1.1%) and reduces CPC 20–35%.** Founder-led UGC dominates supplements (67% of video survivors); fitness video survivors run "outcome-first" hooks <30s. **For SS: Adrian's voice-memo / phone-camera content is the default ad-creative format, not produced studio.** [CONFIRMED — [Reloop UGC guide](https://reloop.so/blog/article/ugc-advertising/), [Brandsearch](https://brandsearch.co/blog/winning-meta-ad-patterns-2026)]
- **Creative iteration cadence: every 2–4 weeks, 3–4 new variations per cycle. Budget allocation: 70/20/10** (proven winners / variations of winners / new experimental angles). [CONFIRMED — [Brandsearch](https://brandsearch.co/blog/winning-meta-ad-patterns-2026)]
