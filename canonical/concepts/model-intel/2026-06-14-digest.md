Exclude them so the optimisation signal reflects true incremental conversions. Directly relevant to SS subscription + OSB repeat buyers. [CONFIRMED — [Flighted best practices](https://www.flighted.co/blog/meta-ads-best-practices), [AlexNeiman ASC](https://alexneiman.com/meta-advantage-plus-shopping-campaigns-guide/)]

### SEO — GEO gains hard numbers; bylines now matter

- **🆕 The Princeton number — content with verifiable statistics + named citations achieves 30–40% higher AI visibility** than unoptimised content. This is now the single most empirically validated GEO tactic. Concrete rules: **direct answer in the first 40–60 words**; **fact density = a statistic every 150–200 words**; **link every stat to its PRIMARY source** (the original paper/report, not a blog summarising it). [CONFIRMED — [Enrich Labs GEO 2026](https://www.enrichlabs.ai/blog/generative-engine-optimization-geo-complete-guide-2026), [Frase GEO](https://www.frase.io/blog/what-is-generative-engine-optimization-geo)]
- **🆕 Off-site distribution = a +325% citation lever, and author bylines now count.** Distributing the same content across a range of publications can raise AI citations **up to 325% vs publishing only on your own site**; **anonymous / "content team" bylines are a GEO penalty** (AI systems increasingly weight author credentials). Per-engine taste diverges: **ChatGPT favours encyclopedic content, Perplexity rewards recency + community examples, Google AI Overviews prioritise existing top-ranking pages.** [CONFIRMED — [AI Magicx GEO](https://www.aimagicx.com/blog/generative-engine-optimization-chatgpt-perplexity-2026), [Enrich Labs](https://www.enrichlabs.ai/blog/generative-engine-optimization-geo-complete-guide-2026)]
- **Carries:** AI search now 12–18% of English informational queries (up from <2%/yr ago); Google↔AI-cited overlap collapsed 70%→<20% (GEO is a distinct discipline, not an SEO add-on); AI trusts communities/third-party sources (Reddit/YouTube/Wikipedia) over brand channels; paragraphs ≤2–3 sentences; data-backed > opinion; 48–72h citation loop if first-party data + schema; derivative content won't be cited; measure brand-mentions + conversion quality, not clicks (AI referral <1%).
