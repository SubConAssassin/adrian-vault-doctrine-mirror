# Venture Launchpad System

## Purpose
The Venture Launchpad is a re-runnable template system designed to metabolise high-leverage frameworks (like niche-audits and client acquisition matrices) into the vault architecture. It ensures that every new venture, the moment it's conceived, runs through a structured, rigorous launchpad pass rather than relying on one-off thinking sessions.

## When to Invoke
- **Idea-stage:** To determine if the venture has a clear offer-object and a viable initial wedge.
- **Pre-revenue:** To define the next immediate form of venture traction (e.g. pilots, LOIs, investor conversations) and map the strategic outreach (Warm/Cold, 1:1/1:Many) appropriately.
- **Revenue-active:** To formally diagnose niche fuzziness, audit current acquisition channels, and pinpoint gaps in the funnel.

## Interaction with Project Ledgers
This system **does NOT replace** existing `_project-ledger.md` files. 
- For new ventures, it **precedes** the ledger by forcing a disciplined validation of the concept and first wedge before formal ledgering.
- For existing ventures, it **supplements** the ledger by providing a focused snapshot of the current go-to-market mechanics and diagnosing fuzziness in the niche statement.

## What it Deliberately is NOT
- It is not a marketing plan.
- It is not a business plan.
- It is not a financial forecast.
It is an operational launch framework designed to define the first/next monetisable wedge and the immediate actions required to secure traction.

## Anti-Patterns and Failure Modes
**Do NOT apply freelancer-channel tactics (Upwork, mass cold email, Fiverr) to luxury, spiritual, or B2B-strategic ventures.**
- *The Cost of Over-Application:* Broad-scale retail lead generation or generic cold DMs destroy the positioning of luxury brands (e.g. OSB) and feel invasive for transformational services (e.g. Subconscious Surgery).
- *Category Mismatch:* Freelancer frameworks are designed for immediate transactional services. High-ticket, pre-revenue, or highly strategic ventures require highly selective B2B outreach, strategic partnerships, and careful narrative building.

## Sharpest Leverage Point
The single sharpest leverage point is defining the **First Monetisable Wedge**. Without a validated first offer, trying to populate all acquisition channels results in scattered, ineffective effort. Focus entirely on the immediate next traction milestone (whether that is 3 LOIs, a pilot, or a waitlist) rather than theoretical broad-scale marketing.
